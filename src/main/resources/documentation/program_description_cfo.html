smAIrtRx - OR

Executive Summary for the Chief Financial Officer

One-Sentence Description: smAIrtRx - OR is a data-driven financial decision-support tool that utilizes advanced bin-packing algorithms to identify operational waste in surgical scheduling, providing a clear roadmap for consolidating operating room usage and reducing overhead costs.

smAIrtRx - OR serves as a strategic bridge between clinical operations and financial performance. By ingesting raw surgical data from your existing Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems, the application applies a rigorous three-stage preparation process (Select, Validate, Clean) before applying constraint-based modeling to separate Physical Asset Optimization (Room Stacking based on Patient In/Out times) from Labor ROI Analysis (Anesthesia Efficiency). It respects critical operational guardrails—such as surgeon availability, procedure-specific durations, and necessary turnover buffers—while mathematically minimizing the number of operating rooms required each day.

Strategic Value for the CFO

The program translates complex surgical logistics into actionable financial metrics:

  • Asset Stacking (OR-Day Elimination): Identifies physical "OR-Days" eliminable through consolidation, directly reducing fixed overhead.
  • Regional CRNA Pooling (Extra CRNA): Calculates labor efficiency across regional pools, allowing CRNAs to float between facilities while respecting the site-bound nature of surgeons and equipment.
  • Anesthesia Staffing (Anes-Days Saved): Identifies site-specific staffing efficiencies through Method 1 (OR Elimination), Method 2 (FTE Reduction), and Method 3 (Gap Absorption).
  • Total Anes (Provider Capacity): Counts unique anesthesia providers across the system at the Site-Day level.
  • Labor Yield (Professional ROI): Analyzes paid anesthesia blocks to identify "leakage" where professional labor is paid but idle.
  • Variance Recovery: Benchmarks procedure efficiency, surfacing outliers taking significantly longer than the institutional mean.
  • Operational Transparency: The Calculation Methodology Summary screen provides the exact mathematical formulas and justifications for every metric in the system.
  • Audit Transparency: The Full Report feature provides deep-dive verification of all savings claims through Java-generated proofs.

Ultimately, the tool enables hospital leadership to move from anecdotal scheduling to evidence-based resource allocation, transforming the operating room from a high-cost center into a highly optimized, high-margin asset.
